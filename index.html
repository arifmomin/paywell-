Know Well

PayWell acts as a one-stop solution for various payments through a large network of outlets, providing users the most convenient way to pay. The retail outlets are available throughout the country and operate long hours to make it easier for users to pay and organizations to sell. You can visit nearby PayWell outlet to recharge your mobile phone, pay utility bills and fees, buy tickets and online products. Our uniquely strong combination of local shops, internet and distribution channels are simplifying payments every day, making it easier, secure and faster!

Frequently Asked Question

PayWell is an organized retail approach by CloudWell Digital Services Limited, focusing on providing convenient payments and value added service to major consumer service organizations in the utility, telecoms, financial services, healthcare, retail, transport, ecommerce, gaming and public sectors in Bangladesh. PayWell intends to deliver payments and services through a uniquely strong combination of local shops, internet and distribution channels. The company follows standard distribution architecture where layers of channel partners are involved i.e. distributors & retailers in its value chain.

You can make payments for mobile recharge, utility bills like electricity, water, internet and DTH, and Indian visa application processing fees. You can also buy tickets and products from online through payment at PayWell outlets.

If you are an end customer, you need to visit nearby PayWell outlet to get one stop solution for your required services. Please go through our services section to know about available services. For corporate customers, our dedicated corporate team is there to provide necessary support. Please contact us for corporate services.

Retailers must have physical outlet/valid trade license, National ID Card and basic understanding of app/device usage. Business types include but are not limited to convenience stores, pharmacies, super-stores, grocery stores, telecom servicing centers, bookshops, photocopy stations, electronic stores and cyber cafés. If want to be an agent of PayWell, you can download PayWell Services app from Google Play Store in your android device and fill up registration form and upload required documents. Upon approval by our verification team, you will get PIN number and can access to PayWell services. If you want to use other means i.e. POS terminal, computer or web panel, please contact our helpdesk for know about manual registration process.

You can easily refill PayWell balance in 2 ways – lift from our local distributor, deposit to any of our designated banks.

No. You can sell any services from single PayWell balance.

Your entitled commission amount is added with PayWell balance immediately after successful completion of a transaction. Service-wise commission rate is different. Please contact helpdesk for service-wise commission related queries.

Management

PROFILE

Anisul Islam

Managing Director & CEO

With the experiences of working with a few of the largest multi-national organizations and after having established a software startup, Mr. Islam conceived and cofounded “CloudWell” in 2012 in a response to the growing opportunity to cater to the retail payment services and relevant support platforms need in the country. Mr. Islam is also a well-known person in the corporate business sector, a fast tracker with a recent career as the Chief Operating Officer of a software company and prior working with IBM, British American Tobacco and Reckitt Benckiser Plc, he has developed necessary skills and competencies in leading organization. His exposure in coordinating international projects and proven leadership in structuring, staffing and managing independent business unit have been the instrumental factors in setting up his own company. His strong network with the leading organizations in FMCG, Telecommunications, Pharmaceuticals and New Technology sectors is also going to be a driving factor for the company towards achieving success in the internet economy model.Mr. Islam attended numerous leadership and project management training locally and internationally. He is completing his M.B.A from American International University of Bangladesh. On a personal note, he often takes part in trekking across Himalayas and is an active member of two mountaineering clubs.
